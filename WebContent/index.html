Music Festival Page

Welcome to the official page of our music festival

Overview

This is an annual outdoor electronic music festival that occurs in March in the city of Munich, Germany.

The festival spans across three days and was founded in 1999. It is held in multiple locations across Munich such as Odeansplatz. It was a one-day festival from 1999 to 2006, eventually making its way to a two-day weekender from 2007 to 2010, and since then has become a three-day festival. In 2012, a record 155,000 people attended the festival. In 2013, for the first time in the festival's history, the festival took place across two consecutive weekends welcoming a combined attendance of 330,000 people. In 2014, the festival returned to its original one weekend format, selling out pre sale tickets in a matter of four minutes. The most recent edition of Ultra, in 2016, welcomed 165,000 people to the festival.

Services

International bands perform every year in the festival.

Stages and booths are all over the festival areas. Booths sell all kinds of products, including souvenires from the festival and from the bands. The areas also offer a variaty of social services.
